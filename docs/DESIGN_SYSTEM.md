# ScholarsNexus Design System

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Deep Navy/Midnight Blue**: `#1a2847` - Main background
- **Warm Gold/Amber**: `#d4af37` - Primary accent
- **White**: `#ffffff` - Content backgrounds
- **Soft Cream**: `#faf8f3` - Alternative backgrounds

### Secondary Colors
- **Dark Navy**: `#0f1b2e` - Darker accents
- **Light Navy**: `#2d4a7b` - Lighter accents
- **Soft Gray**: `#f5f5f5` - Subtle backgrounds
- **Dark Gray**: `#4a4a4a` - Text

### Semantic Colors
- **Success**: `#10b981`
- **Error**: `#ef4444`
- **Warning**: `#f59e0b`
- **Info**: `#3b82f6`

## Typography

### Fonts
- **Headings**: Georgia, serif (elegant serif)
- **Body**: 'Segoe UI', Tahoma, sans-serif (clean sans-serif)

### Scale
- **H1**: 48px (desktop), 36px (mobile)
- **H2**: 36px (desktop), 28px (mobile)
- **H3**: 28px (desktop), 24px (mobile)
- **Body**: 16px
- **Small**: 14px

## Spacing

- **xs**: 4px
- **sm**: 8px
- **md**: 16px
- **lg**: 24px
- **xl**: 32px
- **2xl**: 48px
- **3xl**: 64px

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Gold background, dark text
- **Secondary**: Navy background, white text
- **Tertiary**: White background, navy text, border
- **Hover**: Slightly darker shade
- **Disabled**: Gray, reduced opacity

### Cards
- Border-radius: 12px
- Shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)
- Padding: 24px
- Background: White or cream

### Forms
- Input border-radius: 8px
- Input border: 1px solid #e5e7eb
- Focus border: 2px solid #d4af37
- Label: 14px, medium weight

## Breakpoints

- **Mobile**: 0px - 640px
- **Tablet**: 641px - 1024px
- **Desktop**: 1025px+

## Animations

- **Fade**: 300ms ease-in-out
- **Slide**: 400ms ease-out
- **Bounce**: 600ms cubic-bezier(0.68, -0.55, 0.265, 1.55)
- **Hover transitions**: 200ms ease

## Imagery

### Allowed
- Diverse students learning online
- Tutors explaining concepts
- University-level study environments
- Mathematics, physics, chemistry graphics
- Scientific research imagery

### Avoid
- Childish cartoon graphics
- Generic corporate stock photos
- Excessive gradient overlays
- Flashy effects

## Icons

Use icons for:
- Books and learning
- Graduation and achievement
- Mentoring and guidance
- Science and research
- Online learning technology
- Communication channels
